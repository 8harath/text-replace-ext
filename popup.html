How It Works

TextShortcuts automatically replaces trigger text (like "/mail") with your custom content as you type in any text field across the web.

Simply create shortcuts with triggers and their replacement text. When you type a trigger, it will instantly be replaced with your content.

Privacy Policy

TextShortcuts operates entirely on your device and does not collect or transmit any data.

Your shortcuts and settings are stored locally in your browser and are never sent to any server.
